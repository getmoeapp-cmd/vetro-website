The Cellar

An Award-Winning Wine Program

Wine is at the heart of Vetro. Our cellar holds more than 500 selections from the world's great regions — assembled to complement our creative Italian cuisine and recognized year after year by Wine Spectator.

Recognized by Wine Spectator

Vetro has earned the prestigious Wine Spectator "Best of Award of Excellence" — an honor held by only a small number of restaurants nationwide, and one Vetro has been proud to receive year after year as the sole recipient in Queens.

By the Glass & by the Bottle

Explore the cellar by the bottle, or sample rare pours by the glass from our WineEmotion preservation system at the bar. During happy hour, enjoy select wines by the glass Tuesday–Friday, 3–6 PM.

Curated with Care

Our list is overseen by Vetro's sommelier and built to pair with everything from a plate of linguine & clams to a dry-aged Pat LaFrieda ribeye — with bottles for every occasion and every table.

Dine in the Wine Cellar

Our lower-level Wine Cellar — exposed brick, Tuscan beams, and walls of wine — is also one of our most sought-after private dining rooms, perfect for an intimate celebration surrounded by the collection.

The Space

Chic, Modern & Open to the Sky

Take your celebration to another level. The Rooftop Lounge is a sleek, modern space crowned by a retractable glass roof — open to the sky on a clear night, sheltered and warm when you need it. Floor-to-ceiling windows wrap the room in panoramic views of the bay.

With a stage, dance floor, DJ station, built-in sound system and a customizable lounge, it's purpose-built for parties that move — birthdays, communions, weddings and corporate events alike — all year round.

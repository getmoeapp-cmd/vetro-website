Welcome to Vetro

Treated Like Royalty

As a restaurant, lounge, and catering venue, Vetro offers a modern, elegant setting for all of your occasions. Whether you're looking for an intimate dinner for two or a group of twelve or more, you and your guests are treated like royalty — from your first moment with one of our experienced servers, all the way through to dessert and after-dinner drinks at the bar.

The chef personally creates creative and classic Italian cuisine, pairing traditional offerings with international inspiration, alongside an award-winning wine list with hundreds of selections from around the world.

Indoor Dining

Elegant & Timeless

Our main dining room pairs warm, contemporary design with floor-to-ceiling views of the water. White-linen tables, attentive service, and a kitchen that treats every plate as the centerpiece of your evening.

The Terrace

Dining on the Water

A breezy, covered terrace perched above the channel to the bay — the best seat in Howard Beach for sunset. Open to the air when the weather's right, sheltered and warm when it isn't.

Dock & Dine

Arrive by Boat

Out for a day on the water? Vetro's own marina is open to you — pull up, dock, and stay for a drink or a meal with the bay right at your table. A Howard Beach tradition, on the water.

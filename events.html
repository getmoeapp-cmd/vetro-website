From Our Kitchen To Your Home

Off-Premise Catering

No matter your party size, place your order and we'll take care of the rest — full trays, half trays, and full-service options delivered to your event.

We use only the finest, freshest ingredients — including DeCecco pasta, Carmelina tomatoes, Colavita E.V.O.O., "Plume de Veau" veal and the finest seafood available at market.

Tray Servings

  • Full tray serves approximately 10–15 people
  • Half tray serves approximately 5–7 people
  • Orders of $300+ include bread, butter, grated cheese, plastic serving utensils, water pans & Sternos
  • Wire racks available — $10 per rack refundable deposit

Delivery & Service

  • Free delivery on orders of $400+ within a 3-mile radius
  • $50 delivery fee on orders within a 10-mile radius
  • Long-distance delivery & on-site set-up available upon request
  • Full-service staff — servers, bartenders, cooks & porters — from $30–$50 per hour

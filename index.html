The Vetro Story

Fine dining, reflected in the water

Vetro — Italian for glass — was built to make the most of its place on the bay. The design intentionally blurs the distinctions between interior and exterior, land and water, in a series of richly understated spaces that are all clear light and air, with open vistas to the bay.

The chef personally creates creative and classic Italian cuisine, pairing traditional offerings with international inspiration, alongside an award-winning wine list with hundreds of selections from around the world.
